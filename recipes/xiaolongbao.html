Xiao Long Bao

Note: Image is for food identification, will not be final product

Description

Chinese dumpling with drinkable soup

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • chicken bones
  • green onions
  • ginger
  • pork shoulder
  • garlic
  • white pepper
  • salt
  • msg
  • soy sauce
  • chinese black vinegar

Steps

  1. Make the soup by combining the chicken bones, some ginger slices, garlic cloves, and green onion (sliced in 3 inch pieces) in a large pot of boiling water. Boil for at least 3 hours and extract the soup into a bowl and place it in the fridge. Wait until it solidifies.
  2. Get a cleaver and mince the pork shoulder until it is similar to a ground pork consistency.
  3. Make ginger water and mince some more garlic.
  4. Combine the minced pork shoulder with ginger water, garlic, white pepper, salt, msg, and soy sauce to taste. Cube the solidified broth and add it into the dumpling filling mixture.
  5. In another bowl, make a dough of pliable consistency and knead until soft.
  6. Take a small piece of dough and roll it out into a circle
  7. Add a small but sizeable amount of filling and pleat it towards the center in a soup dumpling shape
  8. Repeat until you run out of filling or dough.
  9. Place dumplings into steamer and eyeball their doneness
  10. Check doneness by opening and tasting one.
  11. When done, serve with vinegar on the side.
