Kamjatang (easy)

Note: Image is for food identification, will not be final product

Description

A pork-bone broth soup rich in collagen. It's flavoured with aromatics typical in East Asian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • pork bone of choice (back, shoulder, neck, butt)
  • water
  • ginger
  • green onion
  • gochujang
  • white pepper
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • potato
  • nappa cabbage
  • white radish

Steps

  1. Parboil the bones and wash out the scum after 20 minutes of parboiling.
  2. Combine the bones, sliced ginger, sliced green onion (3 in. long), garlic cloves, and white pepper. Cook for as long as wanted (min. 3 hours)
  3. In the meantime, peel the potato and slice the nappa cabbage and white radish as desired.
  4. When almost done cooking, add gochujang to taste and the potato whole.
  5. When the potato is halfway done as desired, add in soy sauce to taste and the other vegetables.
  6. Serve in claypot when possible and garnish with green onions.
